All biological rhythms are divided into three groups by the cycle recurrence frequency:
* Rhythms of high frequency with a period not exceeding half hour interval (rhythms of reduction [[muscle | muscles]], [breathing]], biochemical reactions);
* Rhythms of medium frequency with a period of half an hour to seven days (change of sleep and cheerfulness, activity and tranquility, fluctuations [[blood pressure | blood pressure]] and body temperature);
* Low frequency rhythms (change [[metabolism]] in living organisms during the year) are associated with seasonal phenomena: changes in temperature, light and humidity regime
