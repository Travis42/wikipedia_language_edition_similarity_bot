The '''Integrated Authority File''' ({{lang-de|Gemeinsame Normdatei}}; also known as the ''Universal Authority File'') or '''GND''' is an international [[authority file]] for the organisation of personal names, subject headings and corporate bodies from [[Library catalog|catalogues]]
0.242065
It also comprises an [[ontology (computer science)|ontology]] intended for [[knowledge representation]] in the [[semantic web]], available in the [[Resource Description Framework|RDF]] format.<ref>[http://d-nb.info/standards/elementset/gnd GND Ontology – Namespace Document] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130103070757/http://d-nb.info/standards/elementset/gnd |date=2013-01-03}}, version 2012-06-30.</ref>

The Integrated Authority File became operational in April 2012 and integrates the content of the following authority files, which have since been discontinued:
* [[Name Authority File]] ({{lang-de|Personennamendatei|links=no}}; PND)
* [[Corporate Bodies Authority File]] ({{lang-de|Gemeinsame Körperschaftsdatei|links=no}}; GKD)
* [[Subject Headings Authority File]] ({{lang-de|Schlagwortnormdatei|links=no}}; SWD)
* Uniform Title File of the Deutsches Musikarchiv ({{lang-de|Einheitssachtitel-Datei des Deutschen Musikarchivs|links=no}}; DMA-EST)

At the time of its introduction on 5 April 2012, the GND held 9,493,860 files, including 2,650,000 personalised names
