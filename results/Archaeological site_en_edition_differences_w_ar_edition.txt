An '''archaeological site''' is a place (or group of physical sites) in which evidence of past activity is preserved (either [[prehistoric]] or [[recorded history|historic]] or contemporary), and which has been, or may be, investigated using the discipline of [[archaeology]] and represents a part of the [[archaeological record]].
