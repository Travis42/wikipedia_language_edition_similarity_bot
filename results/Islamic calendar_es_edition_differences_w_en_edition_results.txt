The days of the week

It's seven days a week:
* al-ahad (the "first"), Sunday
* al-ithnáyn ('the second'), Monday's
* al-thalatha ('the third'), Tuesday'
* al-arba'a (A.D.A.), Wednesday
* al-Jamís (the fifth), Thursday,
* al-yuma'a (the "meeting") on Friday
