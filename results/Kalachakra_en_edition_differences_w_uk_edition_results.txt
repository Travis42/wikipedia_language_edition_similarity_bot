==Kalachakra Tantra==
[[File:Kalachakradeitydis.jpg|thumb|Kālacakra Deity with consort [[Visvamata]]]]

The Kālacakra Tantra is a key text of the Kalacakra tradition.<ref>{{cite book|author=Khedrup Norsang Gyatso|title=Ornament of Stainless Light: An Exposition of the Kalachakra Tantra|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=IaNcCwAAQBAJ|year=2016| publisher=Wisdom Publications |isbn= 978-0-86171-742-2|pages=i–xiv}}</ref> It is divided into five chapters.<ref>Kilty, G ''Ornament of Stainless Light'', Wisdom 2004, {{ISBN|0-86171-452-0}}</ref>

===Ground Kālacakra===
The first two chapters are considered the "ground Kālacakra." The first chapter deals with what is called the "outer Kālacakra"—the physical world– and in particular the calculation system for the Kālacakra calendar, the birth and death of universes, our solar system and the workings of the elements
