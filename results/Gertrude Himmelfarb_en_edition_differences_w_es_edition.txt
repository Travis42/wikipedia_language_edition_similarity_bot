== Background ==
Himmelfarb was born in Brooklyn, New York, the daughter of Bertha (née Lerner) and Max Himmelfarb, both of Russian Jewish background.<ref>{{Citation | url = http://encyclopedia.jrank.org/articles/pages/4653/Himmelfarb-Gertrude-1922.html | contribution = Himmelfarb, Gertrude 1922– | title = Encyclopedia | publisher = J rank | access-date = July 10, 2012 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160303231847/http://encyclopedia.jrank.org/articles/pages/4653/Himmelfarb-Gertrude-1922.html | archive-date = March 3, 2016 | url-status = dead }}.</ref> She received her undergraduate degree from [[Brooklyn College]] in 1942 and her doctorate from the [[University of Chicago]] in 1950.
