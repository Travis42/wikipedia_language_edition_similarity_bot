==Tōnalpōhualli==
The {{lang|nci|[[tōnalpōhualli]]}} ("day count") consists of a cycle of 260 days, each day signified by a combination of a number from 1 to 13, and one of the twenty day signs
0.0573392
Using the same count, it has been verified the date of the birth of {{lang|nci|Huitzilopochtli}}, the end of the year and a cycle or "Tie of the Years", and the [[New Fire Ceremony]], day-sign {{lang|nci|1 Tecpatl}} of the year {{lang|nci|2 Acatl}},<ref>Crónica Mexicayotl, Fernando Alvarado Tezozomoc p 36</ref> corresponding to the date [[February 22]]
0.0828288
</ref> which are commonly known as ''Xiuhmolpilli'', a popular but incorrect name; the correct Nahuatl word for this cycle is '''Xiuhnelpilli'''.<ref>Tena, 2008:9.</ref> We can see below the table with the current years:
